Basket Weaving

Basket Weaving is the art of taking materials such as wooden planks or wicker and weaving them into the shape of a basket
which can then be used to contain objects or as a decorative item. Baskets are a welcome addition to any interior decoration
scheme and can be very useful. As you will see on this website, basket weaving can be fun too!

On this website, you will find 3 sub-pages that correspond to 3 sub-topics that fall under the basket-weaving umbrella. They are as follows:

  1. Underwater Basket Weaving
  2. Basket Weaving Technique
  3. Materials for Basket Weaving
